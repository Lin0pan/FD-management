## Codebase Patterns
- Domain date rules compare **UTC calendar days** via a local `utcDay(date)` helper (`Date.UTC(y, m, d)`) — see householdComposition.ts and certificate.ts. Never compare raw timestamps; add a named "ignores the time of day" test.
- Domain test style: tiny ISO-string builders (`on("2026-08-31")`, `bornOn(...)`) returning `new Date(\`${iso}T00:00:00.000Z\`)`; one `it` per business rule, named after the rule.
- Boundary tests always cover: day before, day of, day after, plus 29 February (both directions where meaningful).
- `docs/technical_documentation.md` enumerates every module twice: in the source-tree listing (~line 90) and as a `### src/...` section — update both when adding a module.
- Coverage gate: 100% on domain/ and application/; type-only modules (e.g. distributionRecord.ts) show 0% but are excluded from thresholds.
- Ralph batch bookkeeping (archive dir, .last-branch, new prd.json) may sit uncommitted at iteration start — commit it as a separate `chore(ralph)` commit, not mixed into the feat.
